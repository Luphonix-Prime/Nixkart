Returns Policy

Our guidelines for returns, exchanges, and refunds.

Return Policy Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free return policy within 30 days of delivery.

1

Request a Return

Log into your account, find your order, and select "Return Items" or contact our customer service team.

2

Pack Your Items

Carefully pack the items in their original packaging with all tags and accessories.

3

Ship Your Return

Use the provided return label or send to our returns address with your order information.

4

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, please ensure that:

Eligible for Return

  • Item is within 30 days of delivery date
  • Item is unused, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Item includes all original packaging, tags, and accessories
  • Item shows no signs of wear or damage caused after delivery
  • You have proof of purchase (order number, receipt, or account history)

Not Eligible for Return

  • Item is beyond 30 days from delivery date
  • Item has been used, washed, altered, or damaged
  • Item is missing original packaging, tags, or accessories
  • Digital products or downloaded software
  • Gift cards or store credit
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Intimate apparel, undergarments, or swimwear
  • Earphones, headphones, or other hygiene-sensitive products

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will process your refund. The way you receive your refund depends on your original payment method:

Credit/Debit Cards

Refunded to the original card used for purchase. Typically takes 5-10 business days to appear on your statement after we process the refund.

PayPal/Digital Wallets

Refunded to your PayPal account or digital wallet. Typically appears within 3-5 business days after we process the refund.

Store Credit

Issued immediately upon processing and can be used for future purchases. Store credit does not expire.

Gift Returns

Returns of items received as gifts will be issued as store credit to the recipient, unless otherwise requested.

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (damaged, defective, or incorrect item). If you received free shipping on your order, the standard shipping cost will be deducted from your refund amount.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size, color, or product, please follow these steps:

  1. 1

    Request an Exchange

    Log into your account, locate your order, and select "Exchange Items." Specify the item you wish to return and the new item you'd like to receive.

  2. 2

    Return Original Item

    Package the original item with all tags and accessories, and use the provided return label to send it back to us.

  3. 3

    Processing Time

    Once we receive your return, we'll process your exchange request within 1-2 business days.

  4. 4

    Receive Your Exchange

    We'll ship your exchange item as soon as your return is processed. You'll receive a shipping confirmation with tracking information.

If the exchange item is more expensive than your original purchase, you'll need to pay the difference. If it's less expensive, we'll refund the difference to your original payment method.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive an item that is damaged or defective, we sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. Please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving the item.
  • Provide your order number and details about the damage or defect.
  • Include photos of the damaged or defective item, if possible.
  • We'll arrange for a return, replacement, or refund at no cost to you.

For damaged or defective items, we'll cover the return shipping costs and expedite the replacement or refund process.

International Returns

For international orders, our return policy is the same, but there are a few additional considerations:

  • You are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error.
  • We recommend using a tracked shipping service as we cannot be responsible for returns lost in transit.
  • Please mark the package as "Returned Merchandise" to avoid potential customs fees.
  • International returns may take longer to process due to shipping and customs times.
  • Import duties and taxes paid on the original shipment are generally non-refundable.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe:

  1. First, check your bank account or credit card statement again.
  2. Contact your credit card company or bank, as it may take some time for the refund to be officially posted.
  3. If you've done the above and still haven't received your refund, contact our customer service team with your order number and refund details.
