Shipping Policy

Information about our shipping methods, timeframes, and costs.

Processing Time

After your order is placed, it typically takes 1-2 business days to process before shipping. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer.

Once your order has been processed, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information when applicable.

International Shipping Information

Customs Duties & Taxes

For international orders, customs duties, taxes, and import fees are not included in the purchase price or shipping cost. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and will be collected by the delivery carrier or local customs office. The amount varies by country and we have no control over these charges.

Delivery Timeframes

International delivery times can range from 7-14 business days, depending on the destination country. However, orders may be delayed due to customs processing, which can vary significantly by country.

Tracking

International shipments include tracking, although tracking information may be limited once the package leaves the originating country. If you experience extended delays beyond the estimated delivery time, please contact our customer service team.

Please ensure that your shipping address is complete and accurate, including postal code and phone number, to avoid delays in international shipping.

Order Tracking

After your order has shipped, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your order in several ways:

  • Log into your account and visit the "Order History" section
  • Click the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Visit our Track Order page and enter your order number and email

Shipping Restrictions

Please be aware of the following shipping restrictions:

  • We cannot ship to P.O. boxes for expedited or express shipping methods.
  • Certain products may have shipping restrictions to specific countries due to local regulations.
  • Orders with multiple items may be shipped separately if some items are temporarily out of stock.
  • We reserve the right to cancel orders to addresses we suspect are fraudulent or invalid.

Lost or Damaged Packages

In the rare event that your package is lost or damaged during shipping:

  • For lost packages, please wait until after the estimated delivery date before reporting a lost package. Many packages arrive on or just after the estimated delivery date.
  • If your package is lost or significantly delayed beyond the estimated delivery time, please contact our customer service team.
  • For packages that arrive damaged, please take photos of the damaged packaging and contents before contacting our customer service team.
  • All claims for lost or damaged packages must be reported within 14 days of the estimated delivery date.
