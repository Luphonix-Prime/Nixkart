We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $100. For orders under $100, a flat shipping rate of $8.99 applies. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. International shipping rates vary by destination. We typically process and ship orders within 1-2 business days.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also log into your account and view the status of your order in the "Order History" section. Alternatively, you can use our "Track Order" page by entering your order number and email address.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition with all packaging and tags. To initiate a return, please go to your account page and select the order you wish to return. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we'll process your refund to the original payment method, which can take 5-7 business days to appear on your statement.
